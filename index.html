ABOUT US

We are one of the leading suppliers of wholesale heavy power supply products, meeting the needs of end customers. Over the years, we have built a vast clientele list, which includes MEP contractors, commercial architects, and electrical contractors, enabling us to keep growing and delivering quality products. At HIR Electricals, we believe that our company's success is measured by its ability to provide clients with every possible solution for their electrical essentials. As an authorized distributor of Philips products, we take pride in offering ready stock of lighting solutions to meet immediate requirements. To deliver the finest electrical products to our clients, we have strong partnerships with top brands that use premium-quality materials as per industry standards for safe electrical supply. Whether you need a heavy power supply panel for your factory or top-quality wiring for your office, we ensure you get the best and tested products. We have established ourselves in the market by providing the highest calibre products and achieving significant client satisfaction through equitable business practices and superior offerings.
